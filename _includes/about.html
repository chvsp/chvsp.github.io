Why are we doing this?

It can be intimidating on the stage, without a script, especially if you are a beginner.
We have been there. We get it! And that's exactly what we want to change.

All in, as the name suggests is a place for anyone and everyone. Whether you're new, pro, advanced or god, we don't judge or call people by these labels. We are building this space with the intention of introducing improv and its impact on the lives of as many people as we can. It is a result of our personal journeys of learning this art form and also from it.

Our agenda is simple: Every Saturday we come together, learn, surprise ourselves and have fun - all-in.
Rest of the story? We'll tell you at the jam.
